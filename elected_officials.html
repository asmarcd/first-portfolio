My Elected Officials

Live Site
Github Repository

Currently less than 2/3 of eligible citizens are registered to vote, and of those most know very little about most of the officials that they'll be voting for. This Elected Officials Dashboard aims to be an easy place for voters to quickly get information about who their officials are, how to contact them, and, if they've been in the news, learn a bit about their recent activity.
